Graphical Game-Theoretic Communication-Aware Coverage

Two heterogeneous eVTOL fleets operating in an AAM environment with depots and clients for transporting packages. The vehicle agents are operating in a decentralized manner, using a centrally trained graph attention policy. About the simulator: This is a custom developed simulator by me on top of OpenAI Gym, PettingZoo, specifically for improving MARL training performances in mobility applications with hierarchical time-scale and asynchronously decision-making vehicles.
